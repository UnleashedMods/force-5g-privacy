Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

AlexaMods Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Force 5G NR - Network Utility application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

Your privacy is important to us. Force 5G NR does not collect, store, or upload your personal information to our own servers.

To provide network diagnostics, Android requires several runtime permissions. These permissions are used only for the application's features.

  • Location (Precise & Approximate)
    Required by Android to access cellular signal strength, Wi-Fi SSIDs, cell information, and other network diagnostics. Your physical location is never tracked, stored, or transmitted by our servers.
  • Phone State
    Used to display carrier information, signal strength, RSRP, RSRQ, SINR, network generation, SIM information, and device compatibility.
  • Network & Wi-Fi State
    Used to monitor network connectivity, current connection type, and Wi-Fi status.
  • Internet Access
    Used only for Ping, DNS Lookup, Speed Test, and online connectivity checks.

Whenever possible, all diagnostic information is processed locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Advertising

Force 5G NR uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • App usage information
  • Diagnostic information
This information is collected directly by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Force 5G NR does not receive or store this advertising data on its own servers.

You may disable personalized advertising from:

Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete Advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization

Google Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Security

We do not maintain servers that store your personal information. Network diagnostic data is processed locally on your device whenever possible. Reasonable measures are taken to ensure the security of the application.

4. Children's Privacy

Force 5G NR is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective immediately after publication on this page. The "Last Updated" date will always indicate the latest revision.
