Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

AlexaMods Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Force 5G NR Utility mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We build our utility with privacy as a priority. Our application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers.

To provide network diagnostic features, the app requests the following local device permissions:

  • Location Data: The app requests Location permissions (Fine and Coarse) solely because the Android operating system requires these permissions to access raw cellular signal strength (dBm) and cell tower diagnostic data. We do not track, store, transmit, or share your physical geographic location.
  • Network State & Telephony: Used strictly on your local device to read basic carrier configurations, signal strength, and SIM status. This data never leaves your device.
  • Internet Access: Used to connect to public CDN servers solely for computing real-time ping, download, and upload speeds.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you to serve personalized advertisements. We use Google AdMob as our primary advertising network.

Google AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID and network connection data to serve relevant ads. You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

For more information on how Google uses the collected information, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms web page.

3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
